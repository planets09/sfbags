Tucked away in your Rickshaw bag you'll fnd a tag with our special heart symbol, the Rickshaw 'PCQ Tattoo'. This little graphic gem represents three things that are near and dear to all of us here at Rickshaw – Passion, Craft and Quality. The heart represents our PASSION for bags, bikes and our home city of San Francisco.The silver needle and gold thread represent love for the age–old CRAFT of making bags by hand. The five stars represent our dedication to the highest standards of QUALITY. The five stars also honor the constellation of our stakeholders – Customers, Employees, Business Partners, Investors and Community – and our commitment to treat each with respect and compassion. The Rickshaw PCQ Tattoo is just a little reminder of some of the important intangible qualities that accompany our physical products.

SFMade’s mission is to build and support a vibrant manufacturing sector in San Francisco, that sustains companies producing locally-made products, encourages entrepreneurship and innovation, and creates employment opportunities for a diverse local workforce. SFMade was conceived by Mark Dwight, Rickshaw Bagworks Founder/CEO, San Francisco entrepreneur, business owner and resident. Every Rickshaw bag comes with an SFMade label, a testimate to our committment to the manufacturing sector here in San Francisco.

I Make, Therefore I Am. Here at Rickshaw, we're proud of the fact that we make our bags in our own factory, right here in San Francisco. We love designing, making and selling our own products. Our fans are people who care about the story — the 'who, what, where, why and how' — behind the products they buy. They want to know the maker — not just the marketer. They revel in the motivation and the method. We make what we sell because it defines us. We do it because we can. We do it because we want to. And, we do it because it's fun! We believe the soul, and the glory, is in the making. Thanks for supporting our passion.
